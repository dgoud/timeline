1944

Report of the committee on the Training of the Civil Servants

Why constituted?

The need for planned training interventions was felt by the government of the day. Therefore the Committee was constituted to look into it in a systematic manner as the government felt that there should be a planned training scheme for each department under full-time establishment officers for large departments and part-time officers for smaller ones. Hence, there was a need of training in the following domains:

  • Vocational training
  • Background training
  • Further education
  • Centralised training
